Important Kings of the Delhi Sultanate

Kings of the Mamluk Dynasty

The Mamluk Dynasty is also known as the slave dynasty. It was Establised byMuhammad Ghori. Qutb-ud-din Aibak, Shams-ud-din Iltutmish and Ghiyas-ud-Balban are the three great sultans of the Mamluk Dynasty.

Kings of the Khalji Dynasty

The Khaljis continued to use Bandagans or slaves in administrative posts.They also placed people of humble birth in high political and military positions and as governors. Jalaluddin Khalji, Alauddin Khalji and Qutb-ud-din Mubarak shah Khalji are the three important kings of the Khalji dynasty.

Kings of the Tughlaq Dynasty

The Tughlaqs aslo used the Bandagans system. Ghiyasuddin Tughlaq, Muhammad bin Tughlaq and Firojshah Tughlaq are the three most important kings of the Tughlaq dynasty.

Kings of the Sayyid Dynasty

This dynasty was founded by Khizr Khan and continued for less than half a century. The important rulers of this dynasty are Khizr Khan, Mubarak shah, Muhammad Shah and Allam Shah.

Kings of the Lodi Dynasty

The Lodi Dynasty was the first Afghan Dynasty in India. Bahlul Khan, Sikandar Khan Lodi and Ibrahim Lodi are the most important kings of this dynasty.
